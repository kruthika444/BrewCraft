2. Project Summary
   BrewCraft Supply is an enterprise eCommerce platform developed to support both Business-to-Consumer (B2C) and Business-to-Business (B2B) operations for a coffee equipment and specialty coffee supplier. The platform enables customers to browse and purchase products while providing the business with a centralized system to manage product catalogs, customer accounts, quotations, inventory, order fulfillment, and after-sales services. The initial release of the platform targets customers within India, with the architecture designed to support future expansion into international markets.

3. Business Background 
    BrewCraft has been operating offline for years,The company's day-to-day operations are heavily dependent on manual processes. They get orders through call service and check the inventory and warehouse team for stock and prepare quoatation manually. Now these became a time consuming work to do when the business grow or during a peak sale hour, and its prone to mistake and delay in the process. So they have decided to make these process automated and less manal labor requied. After evaluating their long-term business goals, BrewCraft selected Magento because it provides a scalable eCommerce platform capable of supporting future expansion, complex business workflows, and seamless integration with third-party systems.


4. Project Vision
    To establish BrewCraft Supply as a trusted and leading digital platform for specialty coffee products and commercial coffee equipment, serving both retail and business customers. The platform aims to deliver a seamless purchasing experience, streamline business operations, and support the company's long-term expansion into international markets through a scalable and reliable eCommerce solution.

5. Business Objectives
    1. Reduce manual effort involved in order processing, quotation management, and inventory handling.
    2. Automate business workflows to improve operational efficiency and reduce human error.
    3. Provide a seamless shopping experience for both retail (B2C) and business (B2B) customers.
    4. Improve inventory visibility and stock accuracy across warehouses.
    5. Increase online sales and customer retention through an enhanced digital purchasing experience.
    6. Establish a scalable platform that supports future business expansion into international markets.
    7. Enable better reporting and decision-making through centralized business data.
